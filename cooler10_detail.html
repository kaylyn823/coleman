Product Detail

Say hello to the Convoy™ Series Coolers, a collection made for day trips, long-haul adventures, and everything in between.
The heavy-duty Convoy™ Series 28-Quart Portable Cooler is made to move using the nonslip-grip reflective rope handles, perfect for a weekend camping trip or an all-day tailgate.
Every sip should be a cold one, so we added a fully insulated lid, body, and heavy-duty latch.

  • Desctiption

    ADVENTURE READY: A high-performance cooler that's perfect for the campsite, the tailgate, and beyond
    PORTABLE: nonslip-grip reflective rope handles make it easy to move
    SUPERIOR ICE RETENTION: TempLock™ FX fully insulated lid and body keep drinks cold; leakproof gasket prevents cold air from escaping
    DURABLE: Stainless steel hardware and heavy-duty latches hold up on every adventure
    DRINKS FOR ALL: Holds 21 cans with 15 lbs. of ice
    ANTIMICROBIAL LINER: Stain-resistant for easy cleanup
    LIGHTER: 2x lighter than rotomolded coolers
